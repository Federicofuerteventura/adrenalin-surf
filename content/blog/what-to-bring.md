You've booked your surf lesson. You're excited. Now what do you actually need to bring?

Here is the definitive packing list for a surf lesson at La Pared beach in Fuerteventura.

## The Essentials

- **Swimsuit or board shorts**
- **Towel** (quick-dry recommended)
- **Sunscreen** (reef-safe please)
- **Flip-flops or sandals**
- **Water bottle**

## What We Provide

- Surfboard
- Full wetsuit
- Leash, wax, rash guard
